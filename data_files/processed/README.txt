Processed spectral curves
=========================
Files in this folder are resampled to a 1-nm grid and expressed in consistent units.
Columns/units:
  wavelength_nm                 [nm]
  sun_photons_per_s_m2_nm       [photons / s / m^2 / nm]
  asteroid_reflectance          [unitless]
  atm_transmission              [unitless]
  qe_e_per_photon               [e- / photon]
  mirror_reflectivity           [unitless]
